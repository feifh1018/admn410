Policies

Financing:

When you're ready to purchase the yummy food and treats and supplies in your cart, simply click on CHECKOUT button. If you're not logged in, you will be asked to do so. If you are a new customer, click on "I'm a new customer". Registration is fast and easy!

Our checkout is one easy step! Simply enter your shipping address and payment information. We accept Apple Pay, Visa, Discover and debit card. Then, the order will go through and we will provide the best products to the customers!

Delivery:

If your order is over $49, we'll ship it free anywhere in the contiguous US! And don't worry, we don't skimp on shipping. We ship via FedEx, LaserShip, and other premium carriers to provide you with the fastest and most reliable service available. $4.95 Flat Rate Shipping for orders less than $49

Return Policies:

Our policy is simple. If you're not 100% totally, completely, and unconditionally satisfied for any reason whatsoever, return it! We'll happily accept packages within 365 days of the sale date and we'll give you a complete refund.
